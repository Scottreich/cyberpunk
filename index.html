You sit alone at your desk and count the minutes. 7:55am, five minutes before you need to get on the beat.

As you start to lean back in your chair and count the ceiling tiles your trapper keeper chimes a developing news alert.

Millions fall into coma -Neural Crash- News story developing - Is your brain potato safe?
News at 5

Your stomach flapjacks as the fear slowly starts to creep in and shake you to the core.

This is bad , you say to yourself... This is real bad.

The neural link aka "the brain potato" is arguably the most important innovation of the 20th century. No longer is nature the gatekeeper to untapped potential.

The brain potato levels the playing field. It allows users to access, store, and recall any data type instantaneously. Rich, poor,young, old…. everyone has one these days. Most implanted directly after birth.

You can’t help but wonder… Will you be safe?

"Time to begin work !" the trapper keeper chimes as the clock read 8am.

“Same stuff… different day” you mutter to yourself. The last thing you want to do is spend all day writing full pieces. All you can think about is the event the Journalists are calling “The Crash” You reach for your trapper keeper to get started for the day.. When you have a thought… maybe I should reach out to some contacts… see if I can learn more about the crash.

Westwood Park - 6 p.m.

Watchers HQ -- 8 a.m.

A few days later you awake at the Watchers pad to the sound of helicopters overhead. This is bad. Really bad. SCorp is on to you. They must have found out it was the Watchers that leaked the info. You check the Watcher newsfeed.

It’s fight or flight time. Live or die. You climb out the window onto the rickety fire escape and shimmy your way down to your ‘85 Wizzer parked on the street below.

All of the sudden, two black SUVs pull up and block the intersection. Panic races through your body. You turn the bike around and attempt to go the other way, but 4 other SUVs pull up to block the opposite side of the street. The jig is up. You’re geeked.

You dismount your bike, take off your helmet, and stare down your oppressors. Androids in black suits begin piling out of the vehicles. How do so many fit inside?

“People deserve to know!” you shout, “We’re awake now!”

They form a circle surrounding you. There must be 100… They stand alert, silent.
