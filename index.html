Your stomach flapjacks as the fear slowly starts to creep in and shake you to the core.

'This is bad, ,' you say to yourself... This is real bad.

The neural link, AKA "the brain potato," is arguably the most important innovation of the 21st century. No longer is nature the gatekeeper to untapped potential.

The brain potato levels the playing field. It allows users to access, store, and recall any data type instantaneously. Rich, poor, young, old…. everyone has one these days. Most get the implant directly after birth.

You can’t help but wonder… Will you be safe?

"Time to begin work !" the trapper keeper chimes as the clock read 8am.

All you can think about is the event the Journalists are calling “The Crash.” You reach for your trapper keeper to get started for the day.. Then you have a thought… maybe you should reach out to some contacts… see if you can learn more about the crash.

All of the sudden, two black SUVs pull up and block the intersection. Panic races through your body. You turn the bike around and attempt to go the other way, but 4 other SUVs pull up to block the opposite side of the street. The jig is up. You’re geeked.

“People deserve to know!” you shout, “We’re awake now!”

Was it worth it? This is what humanity needed to expose SCorp, you tell yourself. Millions of brains wiped by a blank master copy in the blink of an eye. For what? The potatoes made life better, but now...
